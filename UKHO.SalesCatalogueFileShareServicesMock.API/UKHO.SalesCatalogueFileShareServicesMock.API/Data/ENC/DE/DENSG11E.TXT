NAVIGATION IN BIOSPHERE RESERVE
Non-engine-powered watercrafts are permitted
to navigate Having, Kaming and Zicker See,
keeping a minimum distance of 100 m from
shore.
Engine-powered watercrafts are permitted to
navigate Having from 1 May to 31 October with
a maximum speed of 5 kn.
Other prohibitions must be observed (see Seehandbuch).
