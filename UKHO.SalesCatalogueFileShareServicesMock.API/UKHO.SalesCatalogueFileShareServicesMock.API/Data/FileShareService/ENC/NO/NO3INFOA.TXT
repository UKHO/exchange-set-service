This ENC is not intended for navigation close to the coast and in the 
archipelago, due to scale and generalization. The total amounts of
details are not included. ENCs in better scale must be used when
approaching the shallow area outlined by the 20 meter contour.

Navigation Aids

Lights, lighted buoys and beacons in this ENC are meant as an aid to 
open sea navigation only.
