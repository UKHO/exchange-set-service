Wind farm Baltic 1
Regulations
The safety zone is prohibited for vessels with more than 24 meters length.
The safety zone is prohibited for vessels with a length less than 24 meters
under the following circumstances:
- a visual range less than 1000 meters
- in the night
- wind force with more than 6 Bft
- trawling
Prohibited in the safety zone: berthing, mooring, anchoring.
